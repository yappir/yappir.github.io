Frequently Asked Questions

Can't find the answer you are looking for? You can reach out to our support team support@yappir.com

What is Yappir?

Yappir is a chatbot builder trained on your data that can be embedded on your site.

How does it work?

Give Yappir your website and it will scan the contents. This is then used by the bot to generate the answers.

Does it only works in English?

No, Yappir supports 95 languages. You can have sources and questions in any of the supported languages. No translation required.

Can I customize the bot?

Yes, you can customize the bot to match your brand so blends perfectly on your website.

How much does Yappir cost?

We have different pricing options so you can pick that plan that's best for you. We also offer a free tier (no credit card required) so you can try it with no risk.

Can I use Yappir without installing it on a website?

Absolutely. You can use Yappir to create a private chatbot that's just for you.
