Frequently Asked Questions

What is Yappir?

Yappir is a powerful tool that allows you to easily create a chatbot for your website in just a few minutes. The chatbot uses the content from your website to answer your customers' questions, providing an effortless and efficient way to navigate your knowledge base.

How does it works?

Yappir works by extracting the contents of your website. This content is then combined with advanced Natural Language Processing algorithms to create an intelligent chatbot assistant. When a customer asks a question, the chatbot find the most relevant information from your website to create the response. By leveraging this unique combination of web scraping and machine learning technology, Yappir is able to provide accurate and helpful responses to your customers' questions in real-time.

What kind of content is read by the bot?

Yappir's is designed to extract all types of text-based content from your website, including tables, lists, blocks of code, and more. This means that the chatbot assistant can access and interpret a wide range of information from your website to answer your customers' questions. Additionally, Yappir's AI capabilities allow it to understand the context of the information it extracts, making it possible for the chatbot to provide accurate and helpful responses to your customers' inquiries.

How do I integrate it on my site?

Integrating Yappir onto your website is a simple and straightforward process. After you've added the pages and customized the chatbot's settings to your liking, you can integrate it onto your site by copying the provided code from the settings page and pasting it into the HTML code of your website. This code can be added to any page where you would like the chatbot to appear, such as your homepage or support page. Once the code is in place, your chatbot will be live and ready to assist your customers in real-time.

How quick are answers produced?

Yappir's advanced AI algorithms allow it to generate answers to your customers' inquiries in just a few seconds in most cases. However, the response time may vary depending on the complexity of the question being asked. In some cases, it may take a bit longer for the chatbot to analyze and interpret the query before producing a response.
